Phase 1

Each period, you decide how much to invest. You have a fixed budget of 100 coins each period, from which you freely choose any nonnegative integer amount (between 0 and 100 coins). Your budget comes new each period. As the game proceeds, you will learn after each period how many coins you earned last period as a result of your, and your counterparts, investment decisions.
